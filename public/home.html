Inter-Business Council

About

The Inter-Business Council (IBC), established in 1987, links students, the Shidler College of Business, faculty, and the Hawaii business community.

Clubs

IBC represents student leaders elected to service twelve organizations at the Shidler College of Business.

IBC Events

Attend IBC events through the serviced twelve organizations and get connected with students from the other clubs.
